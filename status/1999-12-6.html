Security
Nov 19
Submitted by Norris Boyd<norris@netscape.com>
Enabled configurable security policies. These allow users to group sites under a named policy and then set what actions sites from that policy will be able to do. See http://www.mozilla.org/projects/security/configPolicy.html.
